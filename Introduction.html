Introduction

Welcome to MyMeetings. We are committed to providing exceptional governance, compliance, and security solutions to enhance your organizational practices. Explore our offerings to learn more about how we can support your needs.

User-Friendly Interface

Our intuitive design ensures that all participants, whether tech-savvy or not, can easily navigate the platform. An organized dashboard highlights upcoming meetings, pending tasks, and important notifications.

Comprehensive Agenda Management

Create detailed agendas with ease. DIGIHUB allows you to set agendas well in advance of meetings, and easily update or modify them as needed. Participants can view the agenda beforehand, ensuring they come prepared and informed.

Document Storage and Sharing

Securely upload and share all pertinent documents, including reports, presentations, and minutes. MyMeetings provides a centralized space for all meeting materials, ensuring that everyone has access to the latest versions.

Secure Voting Mechanism

Conduct votes seamlessly during or after meetings with our encrypted voting system. This ensures confidentiality and integrity in decision-making processes while providing real-time results to stakeholders.

Meeting Minutes Capture

Easily document discussions and decisions made during meetings with our built-in minutes template. Capture key information, action items, and responsible parties, and distribute minutes directly from the platform post-meeting.

Calendar Integration

Sync MyMeetings with your existing calendar to automatically populate meeting times and receive reminders. This ensures that you never miss an important gathering.

Real-Time Notifications

Stay updated with instant notifications about upcoming meetings, changes to agendas, document uploads, and task reminders. MyMeetings helps ensure that every member can stay on track and informed.

Accessibility

MyMeetings is available on all devices (desktop, tablet, mobile), allowing you to manage meetings anytime, anywhere. Whether you’re in the office or on the go, our platform keeps you connected and organized.

Analytics and Reporting

Utilize powerful analytics tools to gain insights into meeting effectiveness, attendance rates, and decision-making trends. Our reporting features allow leaders to assess meeting productivity and make data-driven improvements.
