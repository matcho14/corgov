Our Sources

At MyMeetings, we believe in the strength of our foundations. Our commitment to excellence in governance, compliance, and security is underpinned by a variety of reputable sources that inform our practices and enhance our offerings.

Industry Standards and Best Practices

We draw upon established industry standards and best practices to ensure our platform meets the highest levels of professionalism and efficacy, including:

  • ISO 9001: Our quality management approach aligns with ISO 9001 standards, ensuring a consistent level of service that enhances customer satisfaction.
  • ISO/IEC 27001: We adhere to this international standard for information security management, which guides our data protection strategies and security practices.
  • COBIT: This framework helps us align IT goals with business objectives, ensuring effective governance and IT management practices.

Regulatory Frameworks

We stay informed about key regulations that impact our customers and our platform by referencing and adhering to:

  • GDPR: Our services are designed to comply with GDPR requirements for data protection and privacy for individuals in the European Union.
  • Sarbanes-Oxley Act: We incorporate principles from SOX to enhance accuracy in financial reporting and to maintain stringent compliance measures for organizations listed in the US.
  • HIPAA: For clients in the healthcare sector, we ensure compliance with HIPAA to protect sensitive patient information.

Research and Thought Leadership

To continuously improve our platform and stay ahead of industry trends, we leverage insights from:

  • McKinsey & Company Reports: Findings from reputable consulting firms to understand best practices in board governance and organizational compliance.
  • Harvard Business Review: Articles and case studies providing insights into effective board practices and strategic decision-making.
  • Deloitte Governance Frameworks: Guidelines on effective governance models and compliance strategies.

Technology Partners and Tools

Our platform leverages cutting-edge technology and partnerships to enhance user experience and security:

  • Cloud Security Providers: Ensuring robust data protection measures are in place.
  • Data Encryption Technologies: Providing secure data transmission and storage solutions.
  • Compliance Management Software: Monitoring, managing, and ensuring adherence to regulatory requirements.
